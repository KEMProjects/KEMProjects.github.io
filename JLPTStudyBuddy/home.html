How it works

This website follows along with the Genki Textbookfor learning Japanese. The vocab table shows the list of vocabulary by lesson, with links to Kanji details. The flash cards is an interactive, audio enabled flashcard app where you can study the vocabulary for each lesson, and save specific words to your Custom vocab list. After you are done studying, try quizzing yourself with the vocab quiz. You can either quiz yourself from each lesson, or from your custom vocabulary list.
